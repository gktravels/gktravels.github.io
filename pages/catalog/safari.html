Safari Tours

T

he allure of a safari journey has been with us for generations past. Even the word safari has great origins. The Swahili word comes from the Arabic word safar, meaning to make a journey. And that is what a safari is, really — to make a journey you can make nowhere else. What is now a safari originates on ancient trade routes, only in the late 19th and 20th centuries becoming more of a sporting event. Even then, safaris were primarily hunting trips, only more recently evolving into an educational experience. By participating in a safari, you experience something truly special — something that goes back to human nature and our deviation toward curiosity and exploration. A safari is truly exceptional.
